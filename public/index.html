Esperanto Word Machine

One of the most impressive features of the Esperanto language is the easy building and modification of words and concepts, made possible by it's simple and extremely regular grammatical rules.

This webpage's goal is the practical demonstration of this linguistical feature.
Before the "Word Machine" per se, there's three tables belowfor the prefixes, roots, sufixes and declensions available in this interface.

Prefixes
mal- opposition
mis- "incorrectly"
eks- former / "ex-"
re- over again / back again
pra- primordial / primitive / "proto-"

Roots
pac- "peace"
esper- "hope"
popol- "people" / "population"
fundament- "foundation"
san- "health"

Sufixes
-ar- "a collective group without specific number"
-ad- "frequent, repeated or continual action"
-in- "female"
-et- diminutive
-estr- "a leader" / "a boss"

Declensions
-o characterizes a noun
-a characterizes an adjective
